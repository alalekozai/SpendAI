Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information — your username and password (stored securely as a hash).
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter into the app.
  • Usage data — basic logs such as login times, used only to maintain the security and reliability of the Service.

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive financial account details.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect solely to operate and improve Spendly:

  • To display your expenses and spending summaries within the app.
  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • To diagnose technical issues and improve the reliability of the Service.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to providing the Service.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse.

Your expense data is associated with your account and is not visible to other users. You can delete your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal data with third-party advertisers or data brokers. We may use trusted infrastructure providers (e.g. hosting services) to operate the app; these providers are contractually bound to handle your data securely and only as instructed by us.

We do not integrate with any third-party analytics or tracking services.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the contact details provided on the site. We will respond to all privacy-related enquiries as promptly as possible.
